Disko 1
Easy
Forensics

Given a Disk Image, search through it find the flag.

Hint:
Strings

Steps:
1) `gunzip <zipped disk>`
2) `file <disk>`
	- Revealed that it was a disk image
3) `strings <disk> | grep picoCTF`
	- Reveals the flag in plain text.

Summary:
	A simple flag to find.
